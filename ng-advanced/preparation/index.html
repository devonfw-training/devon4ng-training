Preparation Steps

  1. Please collect topics that challenge you in your current project. We will address them in the "questions from you" part at the last day.

  3. Preparation on your laptops (follow the details in the Community Channel)
  4. Complete the Preparation Exercise between Kickoff and Training (see Refresher Exercise)

Please start a conversation in the Community Channel, if you facing any issues during the preparation or training.
