How to use the reveal.js slides

How to navigate

  • You can use the little arrows in the lower right corner to navigate
  • You can use the arrow keys on your keyboard to navigate the slides
  • You can press escape to see an overview of the slide. Press escape again to go back.
  • Sometime you can slide downwards as well

Speaker View

Some slides have speaker notes.
You can Change to the Speaker view by pressing 's' on you keyboard.

A new browser window should open, that displays current slide, next slide a stop watch and the speaker notes (if there are any)
